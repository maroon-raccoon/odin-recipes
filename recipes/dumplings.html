Dumplings!

No Lunar New Year's gathering or celebration is complete without jiaozi, the steamed dumpling.

Ingredients Needed

  • 3 packages dumpling wrappers
  • 1.5 pounds ground pork
  • 2 cups chopped cabbage
  • 3 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine pork, cabbage, and seasonings. Mix for at least 5 minutes until thouroughly combined.
  2. To wrap dumplings, place a spoonful of pork mixture in the center of a wrapper. Wet the edges of the wrapper with your finger, then gently fold in half and press edges together. Repeat until all the dumplings have been wrapped.
  3. Boil for 6-8 minutes or until floating in pot. Serve immediately.
