Tuna Salad

Tuna Salad!

Simple to make and packed full of protein, this dish is an American classic.

Ingredients Needed

  • 2 cans tuna
  • 2 stalk chopped celery
  • 4 tablespoons mayo
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon dijon mustard

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine all ingredients and mix thouroughly.
  2. Serve chilled.
