Grilled Cheese

The Perfect Late Night Snack

The best things in life are simple. And cheesy. This grilled cheese recipe is ooey gooey perfection.

Ingredients Needed

  • 2 slices white bread
  • 2 pieces American Cheese (ex. Kraft Singles)
  • 2 tablespoons mayo

Instructions

  1. Spread an even layer of mayo of both slices of bread, inside and out.
  2. Place one slice of bread on a pan heated to medium-low.
  3. Place the 2 slices of cheese on top of the slice of bread. Place the other slice of bread on top.
  4. Cook for about 3 minutes on each side. Both sides should be golden brown.
  5. Remove from heat, slice diagonally and serve.
